About Us

We are Batch 25.2, operating as One Humanity (Group G). Our mission is rooted in the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 10 (SDG 10): Reduced Inequalities.

We believe that no individual should be left behind due to their economic, social, or geographic circumstances. Through targeted, community-driven projects, we aim to bridge the gap in digital education and food security, creating equal opportunities for all.
