Who We Are

We are Batch 25.2, operating as One Humanity. Our mission is rooted in the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities.

We believe that no individual should be left behind due to their economic, social, or geographic circumstances. Through community-driven projects, we aim to bridge the gap in digital education and food security.
