The Digital Divide (SDG 10)

Our focus is on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 10: "Reduced Inequalities". The digital divide refers to the unequal access to information and communication technologies. According to recent data, only 18.6% of schools in Sri Lanka had internet access for educational purposes in 2019. By providing a reliable Wi-Fi connection to Homagama President Collage, we aim to ensure that students have equal access to online learning sources.

Partners & Sponsors

In collaboration with Group O (Project 50:50), this project takes a comprehensive approach. While Project 50:50 focuses on SDG 5 (Gender Equality) and provides a computer and a table, ONE HUMANITY (Group G) focuses on SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities) by supplying a chair and a Wi-Fi router. Together, these contributions have established a fully functional digital learning station.

Implementation & Handover

We are proud to announce the successful installation and official handover of the Wi-Fi router and digital learning station to the school.

Community Feedback & Impact

Seeing the smiles on the students' faces makes it all worth it. The school community, staff, and students have warmly welcomed the new digital opportunities, taking a massive step forward in bridging the digital divide.
